Something about me

Hi, as you can see on top, my name is Manuel, I'm 24 years old and I'm born and raised in Argentina.

During my short life, I've tried a lot of different things. From dancing and singing, to mathematics and chemistry; all kind of sports, such as handball, basketball, etc. I spent my days playing the guitar, read books (I'm keen on fantasy/science fiction) and play videogames from time to time. Of course coding is an important part of my life.

In 2018 I started getting involved in the programming world. I did some courses and studied a lot on my own, but I got frustrated, so I quitted. The pandemic changed everything. I didn't know what to do so I decided to give a try once more to programming. First, I did a bootcamp at Henry, an argentinian platform to learn coding. Then I found out about this new program called BrightCode and i felt this was my shot.

Now, the program is ending and I'm about to travel to Israel to work there, it's a dream come true.
